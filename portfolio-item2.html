Tech For Earth

Company Website in development

This website was previously live for some time, however I was not seeing the progress that I would have liked, and decided to take down the website, and revamp it. I ended up decided to code it myself instead of paying for a service such as wix, or domain.com to provide functionality. Sure it has the ability to change certain things, or add html snippets and whatnot, but I saw a lot of things I wanted to do simply unavailable, or required additional spending on upgraded packages for e-commerce for example. Instead I chose to build the e-commerce functionality myself, and it would prove to be a valuable portfolio item to display for clients or employers.

The website is going to have authentication, and backend functionality. I want to have users be able to login and be able to chat with like minded users who are enthusiastic about tech and the explosion of technology in our world. They will be able to watch tech videos together with a chat room to discuss the fascinating tech topics of our world day to day, among other features. Furthermore, I want to be able to have e-commerce functionality and be able to have users add products to their carts to purchase, some external, some sold in house and shipped. Some being third party fulfillment center.
